Things To Do


Resort Activities

Horseback riding. Guided tours. Breathtaking trails, mountain pools and isolated beaches. 3 tennis courts, one paddle tennis court, golf driving range with simulated play, putting green, par course which provides scientifically balanced exercise, croquet courts, horse shoes, shuffleboard, skeet shooting, sporting clays, volleyball court, table tennis and badminton. Or just relax by a beach with a book!






Snorkeling and Scuba Diving

The resort is located on a natural sanctuary with 7 miles of private pristine coastline, so don't forget your swim suits! If you are interested in scuba diving, some licensing and preparation is requried, so please email melandnatemexico@gmail.com for more information. If you are looking for something more casual, canoes, kayaks, paddleboards, and even boats are available to be rented!
